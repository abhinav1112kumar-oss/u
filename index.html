Our Story

Hindustan Chulha Chawkha is more than just a restaurant — it is a celebration of India's rich culinary heritage. Located in the heart of Vivekananda Colony, Adra, our restaurant brings together traditional Indian flavors and modern hospitality to create a truly memorable dining experience.


Every dish at Hindustan Chulha Chawkha is carefully prepared using fresh ingredients, aromatic spices, and authentic cooking techniques. From sizzling starters to flavorful curries and delightful desserts, our menu is crafted to bring the true taste of Indian cuisine to your table.


Whether you are visiting with family, friends, or celebrating a special occasion, our warm ambiance and delicious food make every visit special. At Hindustan Chulha Chawkha, we believe that great food creates great memories.
